# Introduction

This document describes the complete journey of an organization using ENQI for the first time.

Rather than describing screens or technical implementation, this document focuses on the business experience, from the first login to the moment organizational knowledge becomes available for intelligent search and decision-making.

---

# Journey 01 — First Access

A company has just acquired ENQI.

The administrator receives an invitation email and accesses the platform for the very first time.

Instead of displaying an empty dashboard, ENQI starts a guided onboarding experience.

The objective is simple:

Configure the organization correctly before any document enters the platform.

---

## Step 1 — Organization Setup

The administrator informs:

- Country
- Company Registration Number (CNPJ in Brazil)
- Preferred Language
- Time Zone

Whenever possible, ENQI automatically retrieves official company information from trusted public sources.

Examples:

- Company Name
- Trade Name
- Address
- Economic Activities
- Legal Nature
- Company Size

The administrator reviews the information and confirms it.

---

## Step 2 — Organization Profile

ENQI asks the administrator to describe the organization.

Examples:

- Law Firm
- Hospital
- Manufacturing
- Construction
- Accounting
- Retail
- Technology
- Education

This information allows ENQI to personalize the initial experience without limiting future customization.---

## Smart Organization Bootstrap

After identifying the organization's profile, ENQI suggests an optimized initial workspace structure.

Instead of asking the administrator to build everything from scratch, the platform prepares a business-oriented environment based on the organization's industry.

Example:

ENQI detected that your organization operates as a Law Firm.

Would you like to automatically configure a recommended workspace structure?

Options:

- Yes, configure automatically
- No, I prefer to configure everything manually

If the administrator accepts, ENQI automatically creates:

- Recommended Workspaces
- Initial document categories
- Suggested tags
- Department templates
- Initial permission structure
- Business-specific AI recommendations

All generated items remain fully customizable after the onboarding process.
