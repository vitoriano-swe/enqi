# Why ENQI Exists

Companies generate knowledge every day.

Unfortunately, most of this knowledge remains trapped inside contracts, invoices, reports, emails and thousands of disconnected documents.

ENQI exists to transform this fragmented information into organized, protected and actionable knowledge, enabling organizations to make faster, safer and smarter decisions.

We believe documents should not be passive files stored in folders. They should become active sources of intelligence that help organizations operate more efficiently, reduce risks and preserve their collective knowledge.

---

# What We Believe

We believe every organization already possesses the knowledge it needs to make better decisions.

The challenge is not the lack of information, but the inability to organize, understand and connect it.

Documents are not just files.

They are the memory of an organization.

Artificial Intelligence should amplify human decision-making, never replace it.

Technology should simplify complexity, not create it.

# Our Principles

## 1. Knowledge is the Product

Knowledge is the product. Documents are the source.

## 2. Documents are Knowledge

Every document contains business knowledge. The role of ENQI is to transform that knowledge into value.

## 3. Security by Design

Security and privacy are not optional features. They are fundamental characteristics of the platform.

## 4. AI Must Explain

Artificial Intelligence must be transparent, traceable and explainable. Every AI-generated answer should provide evidence of where the information came from.

## 5. Access Must Follow Context

Knowledge should be accessible only to people, departments and roles that are authorized to use it.

## 6. Automation Must Simplify Work

Automation should reduce operational complexity, never increase it.

## 7. Every Feature Must Generate Value

Every feature must help organizations make faster, safer or smarter decisions.
